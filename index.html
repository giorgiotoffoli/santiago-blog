How to Pursue Your Personal Legend

A guide on figuring out your Personal Legend, how to achieve it, and learning from it. Made to inspire people to take their first step.

Step 1 - Consider

Answer this question: What is your number one goal in life? It could be to have a beautiful family, grow rich, become famous, help the world. Whatever it may be, this question can only be answered by you. It is only after answering this question that you can begin your journey.

Step 2 - Plan

After you've figured out what you want to achieve—it's time to actually do it. Firstly, have a plan. If you want to become a doctor and save lives, then know what you're going to have to do: study, get into college as a pre-med, go to medical school, etc. Having a plan with both short-term and long-term goals makes it tremendously more likely that you're going to succeed.

Step 3 - Work

Here comes the hard part: doing the hard work. Following your dreams is hard... very hard. This is the reason why many people fail—it's simply too difficult. So if you really want to achieve your dreams—do what most can't—then you've got to be able to get through the 'dip' in your journey. Only then can dreams arise.

Step 4 - Motivation

There will be a point in your journey where you just want to give up. You'll feel burned out, your very own dream will disgust you. This is an inevitable thing... and also the reason for why you need motivation. How do you get it? Well, the answer is right in your head: visualization. Imagine what it will look like when you finally achieve your Personal Legend. How you'll feel, how your life will have changed. That's how you find your path again.

Step 5 - Achievement

This is it. You've finally achieved your Personal Legend. You've completed your goal and done something that most people can't... but now what? It's easy to feel lost at this point. This is why you have to come up with smaller goals you can always look forward to, but make sure not to make them too big. Creating bigger, unachievable goals will lead to you being dissatisfied once again. Remember, you've done it, now enjoy the fruits of your labor.
